Aalu-Tamatar

Aloo Tamatar Sabji is a potato gravy preparation made with tomatoes and a few spices. This particular dish does not have onion, garlic or heavy spices and thus a satvik recipe. With minimum ingredients, Aloo Tamatar ki Sabji is very simple and easy to prepare, and makes for the perfect food on any day including the Hindu fasting days (vrat). The recipe is also vegan.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Basic Spices

Process of Making

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan
  2. Add half-teaspoon of cumin seeds in the oil
  3. Add 4 medium sized chopped tomatoes and 1 cup of finely chopped ones
  4. Saue till they become soft
  5. Add 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder and rock salt
  6. Stir and Mix well
  7. Add potatoes and let it simmer for 15 minutes
  8. Your dish is ready
